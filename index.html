About Me

Here's a bit of my story

Growing up as a third culture kid in a multicultural city like Dubai, I believe my upbringing has instilled a sense of social consciousness in me. It exposed me to perspectives that were beyond mere nationality and gave me an idea about the global cohesiveness that ties mankind together.


I have spent many sleepless nights pondering on about the purpose of life, those moments of deep reflection have culminated into a single question – Am I living a life of consequence? It is this hunger to make an impact that pushes me through the best of times and the darkest of hours.


For a longtime, my approach to learning has revolved around obtaining a more hands-on experience as opposed to the one-size fits all nature of traditional learning. Hence, I have spent alot of time engaging in projects and research experiences that have inculcated a first principles engineering mindset.


While I'm not programming or building ETL pipelines, I can be found tinkering around with my Raspberry Pi or taking a deepdive into astronomy and politics. We are amidst the digital revolution, for better or for worse, and I hope to contribute to our larger mission by aiding the efforts to create user value by building more efficient, robust and secure systems.


Having said that, please feel free to look around!
